if you want to change how frequent your audio jumps (that skippy time compression you hear on tbs), i recommend copying and pasting this directly into cmd

python audio_skippy.py ^
 -i "input.wav" ^
 -o "output_compress.wav" ^ 
 --target-ratio **** ^
 --frame-ms 15 ^
 --max-chop-ms 35 ^
 --cadence-ms 250 ^
 --crossfade-ms 6 ^
 --energy-quantile 0.5

just increase or decrease some of these, but if you want to be safe, keep it like this
